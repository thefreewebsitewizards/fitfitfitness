Weight Loss Program

Program Definition

Our comprehensive Weight Loss Program is designed to help you achieve sustainable weight loss through a combination of cardiovascular exercises, strength training, and personalized nutrition guidance. This program focuses on creating a caloric deficit while preserving lean muscle mass, ensuring healthy and long-lasting results.

What's Included:

  • Personalized workout plans tailored to your fitness level
  • Nutritional counseling and meal planning
  • Progress tracking and body composition analysis
  • Group fitness classes and one-on-one training sessions
  • 24/7 support from certified trainers
  • Access to our mobile app for workout tracking

Program Duration:

12-week intensive program with optional extensions

Schedule:

3-5 sessions per week, flexible timing available

Self Defense Program

Program Definition

Our Self Defense Program combines practical martial arts techniques with situational awareness training to help you build confidence and protect yourself. This comprehensive program draws from multiple martial arts disciplines including Krav Maga, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, and traditional boxing.

What's Included:

  • Basic striking techniques (punches, kicks, elbows, knees)
  • Grappling and ground defense fundamentals
  • Situational awareness and de-escalation techniques
  • Escape methods from common holds and grabs
  • Mental conditioning and confidence building
  • Sparring practice in controlled environments

Program Duration:

8-week foundational course with advanced levels available

Schedule:

2-3 sessions per week, evening and weekend classes available

Kids Fitness Program

Program Definition

Our Kids Fitness Program is specially designed to make exercise fun and engaging for children aged 6-16. Through games, activities, and age-appropriate exercises, we help kids develop healthy habits, improve coordination, build strength, and enhance social skills in a safe and supportive environment.

What's Included:

  • Fun fitness games and obstacle courses
  • Basic movement patterns and coordination drills
  • Team building activities and social interaction
  • Flexibility and mobility exercises
  • Introduction to various sports and activities
  • Confidence building and goal setting

Age Groups:

  • Little Movers (6-8 years): Focus on basic movements and fun

  • Teen Fitness (13-16 years): Strength training and sport-specific training

Program Duration:

Schedule:

Yoga & Stretching Program

Program Definition

  • Stretch & Mobility: Targeted flexibility improvement
